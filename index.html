How do I capture the weather data in my GHL form? +

                    
StormScan automatically passes three URL parameters to your form: wind_speed, rain, and zip. To capture them: (1) Open your GHL form builder, (2) Add three hidden fields, (3) Name them exactly "wind_speed", "rain", and "zip" (case-sensitive), (4) Save your form. When someone scans their ZIP, these fields will automatically populate with the weather data. You'll see these values in the contact record for personalized follow-up calls.

                

                

                    
How accurate is the weather data? Is it real? +

                    

                        Yes, 100% real government and scientific data. StormScan pulls from:
                        
    
                            
  • Open-Meteo.com - Free weather API used by researchers worldwide
    • 
                            
  • NOAA Archives - Official US government weather records
    • 
                            
  • ERA5 Reanalysis - European weather data (global coverage)
    • 
                        

                        This is the same data meteorologists, insurance companies, and emergency services use. We don't simulate or fake any numbers. The widget fetches actual historical maximums for the past 12 months at any US location. While localized micro-bursts can vary slightly, the data provides scientifically accurate historical trends that create a legitimate reason for property inspections.

All settings are in the widget builder above.
